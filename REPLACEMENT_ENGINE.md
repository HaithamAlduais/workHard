# Replacement Engine

## General Conditions

A calisthenics exercise may replace a gym exercise only when:

1. Movement pattern matches
2. Calisthenics exercise is objectively progressable
3. User can perform minimum reps or hold duration
4. Quality remains acceptable
5. Exercise reaches intended effort range
6. Not merely easy technique practice
7. Weekly volume remains adequate (minimum base volume)
8. Session time stays under ~60 minutes
9. Required equipment is available
10. No significant pain

## Replacement Percentages

- 25% — trial replacement
- 50% — emerging quality
- 75% — consistent quality
- 100% — fully productive skill

## Safety Rules

- Power exercises are not automatically replaced
- Accessories for uncovered muscles are preserved
- Pain blocks all replacement
