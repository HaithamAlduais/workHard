# Graduation Engine

## Contracts

Three templates:

1. **Practical Home Independence** — weighted pull-up, loadable ring push-up, loadable HSPU, weighted pistol, hamstring exercise, calf/grip work, all patterns covered
2. **Advanced Calisthenics Graduation** — strict muscle-up, stable handstand, full HSPU, weighted pull-up, weighted ring push-up, weighted pistol, L-sit, advanced levers
3. **Elite Mastery** — weighted HSPU, heavy weighted pull-up, muscle-up, weighted ring push-up, heavy weighted pistol, full levers, full planche

## Completion Criteria

- Skill unlocked or mastered
- Movement pattern home-ready (equipment, performance, volume, time, pain-free)
- All required patterns covered

## Transition

On completion, generate a four-week transition:

- Week 1: 75% gym / 25% home
- Week 2: 50% gym / 50% home
- Week 3: 25% gym / 75% home
- Week 4: 0% gym / 100% home

Immediate transition allowed only if already home-ready and explicitly chosen.
